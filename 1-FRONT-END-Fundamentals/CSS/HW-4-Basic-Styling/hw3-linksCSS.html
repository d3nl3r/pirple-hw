This is Mark de Rosas' favorite food:
Fried Rice with Veggies

I like the food so much because of its simplicity and healthiness

Ingredients:

  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Green Peas
  • Carrots
  • Spinat
  • Cabbage
  • Chicken
  • Fried Egg
  • Soy Sauce
  • Rice

Steps:

  1. Heat up the oil for 2 minutes
  2. Put the Garlic and onion in and stir them for few minutes
  3. Then Put all of your vegetables onto the fry fan and mix them together
  4. Sprinkle some salt and pepper
  5. Pour a two tablespoon of soy sauce
  6. And put the rice onto vegetables and mix them together for few minutes
  7. And you're done
